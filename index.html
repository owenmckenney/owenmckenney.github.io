The distinctions between scaling laws and curve fitting:

  1. Derived from physical principles (Newton’s second law, Kutta–Joukowski, Euler–Bernoulli).
  2. Units are consistent across all terms.
  3. Each term has a clear physical meaning.
  4. Validity is independent of datasets.
  5. Valid in asymptotic conditions (e.g., zero thrust as amplitude/frequency → 0).
